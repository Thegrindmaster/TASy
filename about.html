Our Story

How It All Began

Every great company starts with a problem worth solving. Ours began when we saw too many promising African businesses fail, not from lack of ideas, but from lack of structure.

Across Africa, we noticed a recurring pattern: brilliant entrepreneurs with transformative ideas were struggling to scale. They had the vision, the product, and the drive, but they lacked the structural backbone that larger enterprises took for granted.

Tehilla Amplify Solutions was born from this realization. We asked ourselves: What if growing businesses could access the same caliber of growth in visibility, strategy, compliance infrastructure, and talent pipelines that Fortune 500 companies enjoy?

That question became our mission. We built TAS around three interconnected pillars, Growtify for growth, Regify for compliance, and WhoFit for talent creating a unified amplification ecosystem that addresses the root causes of business stagnation.

Today, TAS is more than a consultancy. We’re a growth partner, a compliance ally, and a talent matchmaker, all rolled into one platform designed to amplify what you’ve already built.
