Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, reach out directly.

We offer three core solutions: Growtify for digital growth, marketing, and branding; Regify for business registration, compliance, and documentation; and WhoFit for talent acquisition, recruitment, and team building. Each solution is tailored to help your business become more visible, structured, and connected.

Our initial consultation is completely free! We believe in understanding your needs before making any commitments. During the consultation, we'll discuss your business goals, identify challenges, and recommend the right solutions, all at no cost to you.

Timelines vary by service. Business registration through Regify can be completed in as little as 1–2 weeks. Marketing strategies via Growtify typically show initial results within 4–8 weeks. Talent recruitment through WhoFit depends on the role complexity, but we aim to present qualified candidates within 2–4 weeks.

While we are based in Nigeria, our digital services, particularly Growtify's marketing and branding solutions, are available to businesses worldwide. For Regify, we currently specialize in Nigerian business registration and compliance, but we're expanding our reach. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

TAS is a one-stop solution for business amplification. Unlike single-service agencies, we address growth, compliance, and talent under one roof, ensuring everything works together seamlessly. Our personalized approach means you get strategies tailored to your unique situation, not cookie-cutter templates. We're partners in your growth, not just vendors.
